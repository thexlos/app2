# Product Brief

## Product promise

Start Here Helper gives small businesses one easy place to create estimates, invoices, progress invoices, flyers, QR codes, business cards, lead forms, posts, customer messages, and business kits, with real help available when they need it.

Core principles:

1. Run the small stuff yourself. Get real help when needed.
2. Fully customizable, but protected where it matters.
3. Make it once. Use it anywhere.
4. Keep every business profile’s data separate.
5. Start in Simple Mode; disclose advanced controls only when requested.

## Primary users

Owners and small teams in cleaning, flooring, fitness, restaurants, contracting, landscaping, personal care, events, mobile services, and new-business launch work.

## Product posture

The phone/tablet experience is action-based and primary. Responsive desktop adds room for deeper review without changing the data model. The app is a workshop—not accounting software, a bank, or a Canva/VistaPrint replacement.

## Trust boundaries

- Accepted, rejected, signed, paid, and accounting-synced records retain immutable history.
- Customer links work without creating an account but must become expiring, signed, revocable links in production.
- Helper Access uses in-app files, notes, links, and project permission—not passwords.
- Integrations remain clearly labeled placeholders until authorized credentials and production setup exist.
