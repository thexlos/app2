# Five-Layer Correction Audit

This audit maps the five correction pastes received on July 6, 2026 to implemented app surfaces. All external providers remain explicit placeholders until credentials and backend services exist.

## 1. Estimate and invoice system

- Money-owned Estimate and Invoice builders with direct Home, Money, and customer routes.
- Business-specific automatic numbering with manual editing.
- Customer project search, select, duplicate, create, and save fields.
- My Business Kit Item & Service Bank, item snapshots, categories, recent/global/QuickBooks-placeholder entry points, and save-new-item actions.
- Editable sections, ordering, duplication, collapse, visibility, internal costs, profit/margin summary, and custom document columns.
- Customer/PDF/internal visibility controls and internal-only safety defaults.
- Custom payment terms, estimate expiration choices, numbered notes, note ordering, reusable footer, and approval settings.
- Separate customer view and official-document preview.
- Separate estimate and invoice templates, reusable-parts save modal, template detail, and document style association.
- Accepted-scope billing supports full, deposit, percentage, section, selected item, custom amount, and final balance while rejecting unapproved scope.

## 2. Universal starter library and industry blueprints

- Flooring is one business profile, not a global default.
- Broad cleaning, flooring, contractor, gym, restaurant, barber/beauty, landscaping, food truck, event, mobile detailer, general, and custom kits.
- Start with a Blueprint offers Apply Full Kit, Pick What I Want, Start Blank, and Search All Options.
- Global library searches items, categories, discounts, templates, notes, terms, footers, messages, QR starters, forms, tags, payment instructions, and approval presets.
- Saved global rows become business-owned copies; existing records are not overwritten.
- Estimate and invoice suggestions come from the active business workspace and My Business Kit.

## 3. Customers, leads, data movement, schedule, and connected work

- Direct Home actions and working Customers Add menu.
- Separate customer and lead forms; lead detail converts to a customer without retyping.
- Customer detail links projects, appointments, money records, files, help, sync, export, and activity.
- Six-step Excel/CSV Import Wizard with type selection, upload, preview, mapping, duplicate review, and summary.
- Export Updates with full/incremental choices, CSV/Excel choice, record status, and export history.
- Sync metadata models, Ready to Sync queue, duplicate decisions, and conflict review.
- Business-specific Connected Accounts cards for accounting, social, Google, Canva, Drive, contacts, Sheets, Excel/CSV, payment links, email/SMS, lead forms, and print prep.
- Business-scoped Calendar with Today, Week, Month, List, contextual scheduling, reminders, and related records.
- State-based Smart Suggestions with dismiss/snooze/complete actions.
- Clickable Recent Activity with record/area routing.

## 4. Guided builders, document styles, and My Business Kit

- Builder field contracts are the source for one-question-at-a-time guided Workshop sessions.
- Guided sessions persist answers, map them into the real builder, and mark resulting creations as Guided Wizard.
- Estimate and Invoice guided configs reuse the same session/mapping system.
- Simple document style presets plus section-specific advanced tabs for overall, header, info, table, totals, notes/terms, footer, and QR/signature.
- Template detail has use, three previews, style edit, rename, duplicate, set default, and archive actions.
- My Business Kit includes brand assets, website/contact/social/booking/payment/review links, QR codes, templates, document styles, print items, files, item bank, messages/terms/footers, connected accounts, pins, and cross-kit search.
- Logo, QR, and business-card assets open reusable detail actions.
- Money, Create, My Business Kit, File Vault, and My Creations remain separate destinations.

## 5. Start Here Help

- Help remains separate from DIY and guided Workshop modes.
- Eight required service cards use service detail pages and service-specific questions; general help is the fallback.
- Files attach to Help Requests and File Vault.
- Request detail includes service, status, answers, quote, payment, related record, files, messages, timeline, final files, and status-driven actions.
- Completed-work actions can add File Vault files, My Business Kit assets, or My Creations records.
- Configurable Basic, Pro, and Business Partner monthly plans create Help Requests.
- Six practical guides open real pages with related actions.
- Helper Access wording consistently requires files, screenshots, share links, and project context—never passwords.

## Foundation boundary

Imports, exports, sync, email, payment, posting, downloads, and provider connections are safe local mock workflows. Production versions still require authentication, persistent tenant storage, signed public links, file storage/scanning, provider credentials, and server-side audit enforcement.
